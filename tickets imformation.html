Ticket Information

!Please read this carefully, before you buy our ticket!

Accessibility

We strive to make the festival accessible to all attendees. Please contact us in advance if you require special accommodations or have any accessibility concerns.

Customer Support

For any ticket-related inquiries or assistance, our customer support team is available via email or phone during regular business hours.

 

Additional Information

All attendees must present a valid ID or proof of age upon entry.

Wristbands will be provided upon ticket redemption and must be worn at all times during the festival.

Re-entry may be permitted with valid wristbands, subject to security checks

Age Restrictions

The festival is open to attendees of all ages. However, certain areas or events within the festival grounds may have age restrictions or require parental guidance.

Refund Policy

Tickets are refundable "only accept online payment", except in the case of event cancellation or rescheduling. Please review our refund policy on our website for more details.
